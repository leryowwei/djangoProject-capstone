About us


We realise there are a few trip planning websites out there but they often provide poor suggestions for Southeast Asian countries. This is because their data largely focuses on Western countries, thus ignoring the diversity and beauty that Southeast Asia has to offer.

Coming from a Southeast Asian background and leveraging all the untapped information out there on Southeast Asia, we aim to only provide suggestions to places where locals would visit. Our specialised AI planner will curate a personalised trip for you based on your interests and budget.

Why don't you give it a try? It's exactly like booking with a travel agent, but better.
